Welcome to MH Precision Engineering! Your home for quality general engineering services.

At M.H. Precision Engineering we have over 40 years experience in sub-contract machining, horizontal boring, milling, broaching, keyways, turning and fabrication in both mild steel and stainless steel.

Having previously worked at a number of companies, head engineer and owner Paul Taylor made the decision to set out on his own in the pursuit of building his own successful legacy. The company’s objectives are to provide the highest quality and complete satisfaction to everyone of its customers.

Past clients and employers include; Cobble Tufting Machinery, Ministry of Defence, British Steel, Bobst Vacuum, Vacuum Engineering Services, Kelloggs, Delpro, Atlas Converting Equipment, Ash Converting Equipment and Heritage Envelopes.

With a lot of experience and skill at the head of M.H. Precision Engineering, one thing is for sure, the company has a very bright future and the potential customers have an excellent new option for their general engineering needs.
